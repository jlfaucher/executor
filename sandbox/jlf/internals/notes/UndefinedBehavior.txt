#2029 Buil fails with strict-aliasing violations

I tried to build with the following *FLAGS to optimize the build: -flto=4 -Werror=odr -Werror=lto-type-mismatch -Werror=strict-aliasing

The -Werror=* flags are important to detect cases where the compiler can try to
optimiaze based on assuming Undefined Behavior (UB) cannot happen, and miscompile
code that has UB in it. strict-aliasing issues are always bad but LTO can make
them even worse.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The flags are different for clang, an AI proposed :

---
Recommended Clang Set (closest possible)
-flto=4 \
-fstrict-aliasing \
-Wl,-fatal_warnings \
-Werror

If you want maximal UB detection, add:
-fsanitize=undefined
-fsanitize=float-divide-by-zero
-fsanitize=implicit-conversion

And optionally for link-time UB catching:
-fsanitize=undefined -fsanitize-minimal-runtime


Configuation detect_UB incorporated into the builder.
